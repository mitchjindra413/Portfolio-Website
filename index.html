About

I am Mitchell Jindra

Striving for improvement one line of code at a time.

I am a full-stack software engineer with experience in React, Redux, Rails, Express, Node, and Java with Spring Boot. I thrive on solving complex problems, building scalable solutions, and continuously learning new technologies.

I previously worked at Tutoria, an EdTech company where I developed tools to help people improve their English skills, and at SmoothStack, where I gained experience in backend development, cloud infrastructure, and full-stack engineering. My background in teaching and international business has shaped my ability to communicate effectively, adapt to new challenges, and approach problem-solving from multiple perspectives. What I love most about software engineering is the ability to take an idea, iterate on it, and turn it into something tangible that people can use. I enjoy working on collaborative teams, refining systems for efficiency, and writing clean, maintainable code.

On a less serious note, I'm a huge movie and music fanatic (Spotify wrapped is my favorite time of year - probably why I made a personal version). I also love snowboarding! However, growing up on the flat plains of Nebraska has limited the number of times I've been able to practice. I've got left turns down, though right turns, not so much!
